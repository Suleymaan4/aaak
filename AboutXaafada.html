ku soo dhawaaw Xaafada Oodwene , hadii aan wax yar idin kaga sheego xaafadayda oo ladhaho Oodweyne,
xaafada Oodweyne waxay ku taaal degmada deyniile taas oo ah degmada ugu weyne degmooyinka gobolka banaadir

Xaafada Oodweyne waa xaafad ku taal cirifka degmada deyniile oo leh koboc fiican dhan kasta
midakale waaa xaafada mar dhaw la aasaay oo ku soo karay degmada laakin dadkeeda ayaa aad u hormariyay
waaa xaafad aad dadka ugu soo badanaayaan waxaana sababaaya waxayaabo fara badan oo kala ah :

1. Amniga oo wanaagsan
2. Hawadeyda oo nadiif ah
3. nolosha oo jaban
4. Xaafada oo koreysa
